Grace Chung enjoys long walks in the mountains, cuddling her dog Georgie, and clean and simple design.

  • Her work combines fashion merchandising with whimsy and user-centered design thinking. Grace currently works at EPIC Antique, where she conducts all phases of the merchandising process, from floor moves and closing sales to sales and data analyses. She will also be designing their very first website.

    Grace received her BA in International Relations from the University of California-Irvine in 2009. Her foundation in inter-cultural communication informs her work as a UX designer. She continued her studies at FIDM-Los Angeles, receiving her AA in Merchandise Marketing. She continues to be an avid consumer of art and design as well as hopes to do greater work in the latter.
